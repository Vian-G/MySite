ANIMATION APPROACH
==================================================

Use a robust hybrid approach. Do not force unrelated shapes into a direct SVG morph if it looks distorted.

Implement:
1. One shared continuous trajectory path that persists through all four states

3. Crossfade project-specific SVG layer groups in and out
4. Transform animation for robot-arm segments, rotating around their joints
5. Subtle opacity and line-draw transitions for sensor and flight details
6. Shared rust-red trajectory should visibly carry the eye from one discipline to the next
